Breathing Techniques

1. Diaphragmatic Breathing (Belly Breathing)

Use: Helps to reduce stress, improve focus, and lower blood pressure.
How: Inhale deeply through your nose so your belly rises. Exhale slowly through your mouth.

2. Box Breathing

Use: Useful for improving mental clarity and calming the nervous system.
How: Inhale for 4 counts, hold your breath for 4 counts, exhale for 4 counts, and hold for 4 counts. Repeat.

3. Alternate Nostril Breathing

Use: Promotes relaxation and balances the body and mind.
How: Close your right nostril with your thumb, inhale through the left nostril. Close the left nostril, exhale through the right nostril. Repeat.

4. 4-7-8 Breathing

Use: Great for falling asleep quickly and calming anxiety.
How: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 7 counts, and exhale slowly for 8 counts. Repeat.
